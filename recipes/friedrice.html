Fried Rice Recipe

A delicious meal on a budget!

This recipe will teach you a delicious fried rice recipe that's easy to prepare and easy on the wallet!

Ingredients and Required Tools

  • 1 cup of frozen chickpeas
  • Refrigerated rice
  • 1/2 cup of chopped carrots
  • 2 large eggs
  • Soy sauce to taste
  • Some vegetable oil, preferably sesame seed oil
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • A wok

Instructions

  1. Scramble eggs into a large wok, breaking them up gradually. Transfer to a seperate plate and allow to cool
  2. Add some oil to the pan and then sautee veggies and garlic until soft. Transfer to a separate plate and allow to cool
  3. Add oil to pan, and then add veggies, soy sauce and rice. Stir fry until light brown
  4. Stir in eggs, add some extra oil, and gradually season with salt and pepper
  5. Plate and serve!
