Protein Shake Recipe

A healthy drink to fuel you after a workout!

This recipe will teach you a delicious chocolate strawberry banana smoothie that is both easy to make and great for you!

Ingredients and Required Tools

  • 250 g of frozen strawberries
  • 1 large ripe banana
  • 100 g of chocolate flavored protein powder
  • 200 mL of oat milk
  • Some ice cubes
  • A blender

Instructions

  1. Peel the banana and chop it into thumb-sized chunks
  2. Measure out the oat milk and pour it into a blender
  3. Add all the fruits and the protein powder
  4. Add in the water
  5. Add in ice cubes to preference - changing the amount of cubes will change the consistency of the shake, so make sure to test it out!
  6. Blend, and enjoy!
